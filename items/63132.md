## Summary

Current `main` has shipped support for `models.providers.*.request.allowPrivateNetwork` on the audio transcription path, which resolves the reported SSRF regression for trusted self-hosted STT endpoints. The changelog records the fix in `v2026.4.15`, the config schema exposes the setting, and the media-understanding regression test covers the audio path.

## Evidence
