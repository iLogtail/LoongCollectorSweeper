## Summary

Keep open. Current main still lacks a sanitizer for bare OpenAI-style `{"tool_calls":[...]}` text in the implicated final/visible-text helper and partial preview path. The two related fix PRs, #71954 and #72011, were closed unmerged, so they do not supersede or prove the issue is shipped.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #70797 open and land a focused fix in the shared assistant-visible-text sanitizer plus the Telegram/direct partial-preview path. #72011 is the closest design reference: add streaming-aware withholding/dropping for standalone `{"tool_calls":[...]}` JSON, preserve normal JSON/code examples, and add regressions for final cleanup and partial preview delivery.
