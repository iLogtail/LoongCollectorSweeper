## Summary

Keep open. Current main has cost/usage reporting and per-agent session disk-budget enforcement, but it does not implement config-driven daily/monthly per-agent spend caps or a gateway-level blocking check before each model call. The related comments are useful: ATXP, nornr.com, and vibe-billing describe external/proxy approaches, and the LiteLLM docs show provider/proxy spend-limit workarounds, but those do not satisfy the requested native gateway enforcement and do not prove this belongs solely in ClawHub/plugin space because the current model-call hooks are observation-oriented rather than a blocking per-call budget gate.
