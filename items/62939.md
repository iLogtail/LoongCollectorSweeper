## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has partial prompt-injection hardening for untrusted metadata, fetched/file content, and VoiceClaw async tool updates, but the generic transport path still forwards raw user bodies and raw `toolResult` text to providers without the requested structural trust delimiters at message/tool-result boundaries.
