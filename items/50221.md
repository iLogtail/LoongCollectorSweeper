## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has already landed part of this PR's scope (`/exec` and verbose persistence gating for internal non-admin callers), but the PR's `chat.send` hardening is not on `main`, and the review-thread P1 remains valid: changing `CommandAuthorized` to false for `operator.write` callers would also block intentionally allowed read-only internal commands like `/status`, `/whoami`, and `/config show` unless command-level scope metadata is added first. The plugin-runtime config-write sink hardening from the PR is also still absent on `main`.

- Current `main` still relies on per-command internal scope gates plus `GatewayClientScopes`; the PR's blanket `chat.send` authorization change remains unresolved and would likely regress allowed read-only flows.
