## Summary

Keep open. Current main appears to have addressed some sub-findings from the field report, but the issue as a whole is not strongly closeable: config-key cleanup and cron retry/docs improved, yet the onboarding token-mismatch concern still appears unresolved and the cron JSON-shape documentation called out in the report is still not clearly documented on main.
