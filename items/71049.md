## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still hardcodes the Copilot-style informative DM stream for Microsoft Teams personal chats, and the exposed config/schema does not provide a DM-specific opt-out or fallback-to-native-typing mode.

## Evidence

- **Personal chats always create the Teams stream controller and send an informative update:** `createTeamsReplyStreamController` constructs `TeamsHttpStream` whenever `conversationType === "personal"`, and `onReplyStart()` unconditionally sends `sendInformativeUpdate(...)` once for that stream.
  - file: [extensions/msteams/src/reply-stream-controller.ts:34](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/bc21f500d4c14d8ecbacb992c39c7ff0954b04f2/extensions/msteams/src/reply-stream-controller.ts#L34)
  - sha: [bc21f500d4c1](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/bc21f500d4c14d8ecbacb992c39c7ff0954b04f2)
- **`typingIndicator` only gates native typing keepalive, not the DM informative stream:** `createMSTeamsReplyDispatcher` always calls `streamController.onReplyStart()` first, then only conditionally starts `typingCallbacks.onReplyStart()` when `typingIndicatorEnabled` is true.
  - file: [extensions/msteams/src/reply-dispatcher.ts:256](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/bc21f500d4c14d8ecbacb992c39c7ff0954b04f2/extensions/msteams/src/reply-dispatcher.ts#L256)

  - file: [src/config/types.msteams.ts:134](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/bc21f500d4c14d8ecbacb992c39c7ff0954b04f2/src/config/types.msteams.ts#L134)

  - file: [src/config/zod-schema.providers-core.ts:1577](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/bc21f500d4c14d8ecbacb992c39c7ff0954b04f2/src/config/zod-schema.providers-core.ts#L1577)
