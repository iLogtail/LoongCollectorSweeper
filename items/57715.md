## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `c65aa1d2a6e573d49619c4dd0698a76010476e7d` still builds full session rows before `search`, `activeMinutes`, and `limit` are applied, and each row build can still trigger transcript fallback reads plus a full-store child-session scan. That matches the reported `sessions.list` hot-path bug, and I found no landed main-branch proof that the linked early-filter/request-scoped child-index fix has shipped.
