## Summary

Keep open. Current main still acknowledges Telegram webhook requests before `bot.handleUpdate` completes, and I found no disk-backed inbound webhook queue or startup replay implementation. The related fix PRs in the provided context (#40056, #42185, #42523) are all closed and unmerged, so they do not supersede the open issue.
