## Summary

Keep open. Current main still serializes each session lane, only exposes global and subagent concurrency config, and has no config-backed auto-yield or lane-wait warning threshold. The existing steer/interrupt modes and manual `sessions_yield` narrow the issue, but they do not satisfy the requested configurable session concurrency or automatic yielding.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open and narrow the product path: add a documented, schema-backed session-lane concurrency setting applied to `session:<key>` lanes, decide whether auto-yield belongs in the embedded runner/tool loop or queue policy, and wire any lane wait warning threshold through config/types/schema/docs/tests. PR #63864 may inform the concurrency part, but it should not close this issue unless it is corrected, merged, and accompanied by follow-up tracking for auto-yield and warning-threshold configurability.
