## Summary

Current `main` still shows the reported Discord thread-starter reinjection path, and the reply pipeline has explicit follow-up-turn behavior that prepends `[Thread starter - for context]` when thread history is absent. I did not find evidence that issue #41355 is fixed on `main` or shipped in `v2026.4.22`.
