## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has independently absorbed some of the PR’s provider-aware context-window work, but it has not fully implemented the PR: `src/commands/status.summary.ts` still lets persisted `entry.contextTokens` override fresh model resolution in session rows, and the centralized `src/agents/model-metadata.ts` layer/docs described by the PR are not present on `main`. That makes the PR partially stale, not safely closeable.
