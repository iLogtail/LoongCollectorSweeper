## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` includes part of the discussed CLI-backend ownership/runtime work, but the narrowed bug in this issue still exists: the three helper surfaces named in the issue still call the embedded runner directly instead of dispatching through `runCliAgent()` for CLI-backed providers.
