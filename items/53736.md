## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement the proposed `tools.exec.denyPatterns` config or runtime enforcement, and the existing exec controls (`tools.deny`, `tools.exec.security`, approvals, `safeBins`) are adjacent but not equivalent to a regex-based deny guard.

## Evidence
