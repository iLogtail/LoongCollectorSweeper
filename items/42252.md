## Summary

Keep open. Current main has useful related diagnostics, including gateway status summaries, service config drift auditing, and Telegram duplicate-poller log hints, but the specific doctor UX requested in #42252 is not implemented: doctor still runs gateway health and daemon/service checks as separate steps, can still emit `Gateway not running` from the health probe and later service runtime details from the daemon flow, has no consolidated final health verdict, and exposes no doctor machine-readable diagnosis block.

## Evidence
