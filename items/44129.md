The remaining WSL skill-loading bug is real enough to keep tracked, but this PR is not a conservative auto-close candidate because its proposed fix bypasses managed-skill containment broadly and it bundles unrelated Feishu and sandbox changes.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the PR open for maintainer review or ask the author to split it. The Feishu close-race and sandbox permission-preservation changes should move to separate PRs or be explicitly tied to their own issues and tests.
