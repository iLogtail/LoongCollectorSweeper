## Summary

Keep open. Current main still does not add `cron` to the internal hook event contract or bridge CronService lifecycle events into the internal `HOOK.md` hook system. Cron lifecycle events exist and are broadcast by the gateway, but they are not dispatched through `createInternalHookEvent`, `triggerInternalHook`, or `fireAndForgetHook`. Related implementation PRs #37881 and #37885 are closed unmerged, and the later same-scope issue #47903 is closed rather than a canonical open tracker.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #37855 open as the canonical internal-hooks request. The best implementation is an additive core change: add a typed `cron` internal hook event based on `CronEvent`, emit it from `src/gateway/server-cron.ts` with `createInternalHookEvent` plus `fireAndForgetHook` or equivalent non-blocking error handling, and cover `added`, `updated`, `removed`, `started`, and `finished` in focused hook and gateway tests. If maintainers want cron observability to be plugin-only, that needs an explicit product/API decision and docs explaining why `HOOK.md` cron events are out of scope.
