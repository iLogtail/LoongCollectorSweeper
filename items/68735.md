## Summary

Keep open. Current main contains strong fixes for important subcases: DeepSeek V4 reasoning replay, Copilot encrypted/reasoning item replay, and Codex-named Copilot forward-compat. However the issue discussion is broader than those fixed subcases, and the maintainer comment explicitly left #68735 open for non-DeepSeek and broader `github-copilot/*` triage. I do not see enough source/test evidence to close the original `github-copilot/gpt-5-mini`/`raptor-mini` report or the later vLLM empty-tools schema rejection as fully resolved.
