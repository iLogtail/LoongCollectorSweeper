## Summary

Keep open. Current main still has a write-ahead outbound delivery queue that is recovered on gateway startup without any stale-age/TTL filter or restart-generation invalidation. Adjacent restart-continuation work is bounded separately, but that does not cover the general outbound queue described in this Telegram restart replay report.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open and implement a conservative outbound delivery-queue freshness policy in core, likely in `src/infra/outbound/delivery-queue-recovery.ts` plus config/schema/docs/tests. The policy should preserve brief crash recovery but drop, quarantine, or fail old entries on startup based on a configurable max age, with regression coverage for Telegram/forum-style routed payloads and for existing fresh crash-replay behavior.
