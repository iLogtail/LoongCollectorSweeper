## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has generic unsupported-keyword stripping infrastructure, but the bundled BytePlus/Volcengine provider paths do not declare `unsupportedToolSchemaKeywords` or provider tool-compat hooks, so `patternProperties` is still not stripped for those providers on the normal tool-registration path.

## Evidence

- **Schema cleanup is still gated on Gemini or explicit model compat:** `normalizeToolParameterSchema()` only calls `cleanSchemaForGemini()` for Google/Gemini providers, and otherwise strips keywords only when `modelCompat.unsupportedToolSchemaKeywords` is populated.
