## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still leaves `HEARTBEAT_OK` untouched when it appears in the middle of a heartbeat reply, and the public docs still describe that exact limitation. The PR’s extra transcript-policy/minimax plumbing is partly obsolete on `main`, but the heartbeat fix itself is not implemented there.
