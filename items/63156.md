## Summary

Current `main` now routes slow/no-output model timeouts through the embedded-runner idle-timeout logic, which inherits `agents.defaults.timeoutSeconds` when `agents.defaults.llm.idleTimeoutSeconds` is unset. That addresses the issueâ€™s core request to stop failing at a fixed 15s for slow local providers such as LM Studio.
