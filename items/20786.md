## Summary

Keep #20786 open. Current main still only allows Telegram Business update names through polling/webhook selection; it does not process `business_message` or `business_connection`, does not dedupe or lane those payloads, does not attach `business_connection_id` to outbound replies, and has no user-facing Telegram Business docs/tests. PR #53629 remains the relevant open implementation attempt rather than shipped main behavior.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #20786 open as the canonical Telegram Business support tracker. Land PR #53629 or an equivalent `extensions/telegram` implementation that registers `business_connection` and `business_message` handlers, routes business messages through the existing inbound pipeline, handles dedupe and sequential keys, persists/maps `business_connection_id` for outbound replies while excluding unsupported calls such as `sendChatAction`, and adds focused tests plus user-facing docs at https://docs.openclaw.ai/channels/telegram.
