## Summary

Current main does not implement the requested Telegram Business behavior. The Telegram plugin allows business update types through polling/webhook, but there are still no business_message or business_connection handlers, dedupe does not model business update payloads, and the linked PR #53629 is open/unmerged rather than a landed fix. Keep the issue open until #53629 or equivalent work lands.
