## Summary

Keep #20786 open. Current main still only allows Telegram Business update types through `allowed_updates`; it does not register handlers for `business_message` or `business_connection`, does not route business messages through the inbound pipeline, and has no outbound `business_connection_id` support. The provided discussion also identifies #20786 as the canonical tracker, with PR #53629 still open as implementation context rather than landed evidence.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #20786 open as the canonical Telegram Business support tracker. Land PR #53629 or an equivalent Telegram plugin change that registers `business_connection` and `business_message` handlers, routes business messages through the existing inbound pipeline, adds dedupe/sequential-key support, persists or maps `business_connection_id`, injects it for outbound business replies while excluding unsupported API calls such as `sendChatAction`, and adds focused tests plus user-facing Telegram docs.
