## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has partial mitigation for malformed Kimi tool-call arguments and already simplified the read-tool validation label to `path`, but the Kimi provider still explicitly disables replay signature preservation and tests lock that behavior in. That is not strong evidence that the reported infinite-loop regression is fixed on `main`.
