## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still retains successful compaction snapshot `.checkpoint.*.jsonl` files on disk, trims only the in-store `compactionCheckpoints` metadata, and relies on separate disk-budget cleanup rather than deleting snapshots when they age out of the checkpoint window. One supplemental sub-claim in the thread appears outdated on current `main`: checkpoint tracking now stores `preCompaction.sessionFile` rather than a `checkpointFile: null` field.
