## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not contain this PR's behavior: Discord upload blobs are still created without a MIME type, and the shared web-media loader still only skips optimization for GIFs rather than preserved WebP/AVIF or other animated image cases. The requested fix is therefore not implemented on `main` and should not be closed as already handled.
