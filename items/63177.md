## Summary

Heartbeat already runs in the main session by default, persists substantive heartbeat turns into transcript history, and only filters no-op `HEARTBEAT_OK` pairs out of future context assembly. The requested behavior is already present on current `main` and was already shipped in `v2026.4.23`; the opt-out for no-history heartbeats is `heartbeat.isolatedSession: true`.
