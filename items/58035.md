## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still drops exec/tool-result stdout-stderr whenever a later assistant summary exists, and it still fails to extract nested `{ content: [...] }` tool-result payloads. The PR’s requested behavior is not implemented on `main` as of `90877e0d4274c53fd91bf5787265c4d6889dc9a7`.
