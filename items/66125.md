## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` includes partial OpenAI-compatible proxy hardening, but the broader reported failure remains unverified and unresolved: maintainer comments on April 25, 2026 explicitly say the issue stays open pending an exact failing proxy request/response capture, and current fallback diagnostics still only retain summarized error metadata rather than full request/response detail.
