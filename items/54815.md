## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not include a Bocha web-search provider, Bocha docs, or `BOCHA_API_KEY` support, so this is not implemented on main. The repo also still ships and documents new bundled `web_search` providers on `main`, so there is not strong evidence to close this as a ClawHub-only request.
