## Summary

Current main still has the trusted-proxy loopback/internal-client failure shape reported in #43300. The related fixes/RFC are open and useful, but not merged, so this should remain open rather than be closed as implemented.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #43300 open until maintainers choose and land the trusted-proxy internal-client fix. The near-term implementation likely belongs in the gateway auth and credential path covered by #64122 and/or #63379, with tests in `src/gateway/auth.test.ts`, `src/gateway/call.test.ts`, and `src/gateway/credentials.test.ts`; the longer-term design should follow the service-identity discussion in #69066.
