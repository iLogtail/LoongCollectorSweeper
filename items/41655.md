## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` already replaced the skills-side watcher with a narrower `SKILL.md`-only watch, so that half of the PR is obsolete, but the memory-side change proposed here is not on `main`: the memory watcher still recursively watches `memory/` plus `extraPaths` globs with no depth cap or equivalent code-level fix.
