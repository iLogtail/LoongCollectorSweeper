## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement the proposed `sessions_send` JSONL comms log, and the existing observability on `main` is still limited to returned tool status plus inter-session transcript provenance. The PR remains additive rather than obsolete.

## Evidence

That means the proposed JSONL audit trail is not implemented on `main`.
