## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` appears to have independently landed only the `config.openFile` shell-safety slice; the browser-eval filter, loopback auth-throttle change, extra default header changes, and the entire `src/security/shield/*` subsystem are not present on `main`, and the PR discussion still shows unresolved correctness concerns beyond the REOPEN fix.
