## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` only partially covers the request: it strips trailing `NO_REPLY` and glued leading prefixes, but the documented/runtime contract is still exact-token-only for suppression, and delivery tests intentionally preserve mixed `NO_REPLY` text instead of suppressing whitespace-separated prefix/suffix variants gateway-wide.
