## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still has the reported bug, and the PR's latest patch appears to introduce an upgrade-path regression for users whose tracked template was already mutated by the old setup flow. That makes auto-closing inappropriate and the PR still potentially useful, but not yet safe to treat as resolved.
