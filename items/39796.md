## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement the PR’s round-trip `pingPresence()` approach, and the underlying quiet-channel restart behavior still exists through the WhatsApp watchdog path. The original `lastEventAt`/gateway-health explanation is partly stale because `main` now gates `stale-socket` on `lastTransportActivityAt`, but WhatsApp still reconnects after 30 minutes with no inbound traffic.
