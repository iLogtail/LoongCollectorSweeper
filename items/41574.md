## Summary

Current main already carries the WebChat/Control UI TTS attachment fix: audio-only assistant replies are preserved, assistant `MEDIA:` audio directives normalize into structured audio attachments, and the Control UI renders them with an `<audio>` player. The changelog shows this shipped in v2026.4.11, with additional webchat audio-history hardening in v2026.4.22.
