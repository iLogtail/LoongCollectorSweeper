## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still limits generic `/think` choices to `off|minimal|low|medium|high` unless a provider supplies a custom thinking profile, and the bundled Ollama provider does not advertise one. The issue body's claim that config rewrites strip `compat.supportedReasoningEfforts` is not supported by current source, but the local `/think max` rejection remains unresolved for Ollama.
