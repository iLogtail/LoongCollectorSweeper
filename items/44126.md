## Summary

Current `main` does not contain the PR’s agent-side adaptive thinking controls. OpenClaw today supports user/session `/think` directives, but the proposed `set_thinking_level` tool, adaptive-thinking system-prompt section/gating, and live per-request thinking propagation through provider wrappers are still absent on `main`, so this PR should remain open.
