## Summary

Keep open. Current main deletes fresh successful one-shot `at` jobs with `deleteAfterRun`, but the scheduler guard discussed in #56488 is still not applied on the normal timer or `runDueJobs` paths. The open PR #56509 remains the direct fix candidate for terminal one-shot jobs with stale past `nextRunAtMs` values.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #56488 open until #56509 or an equivalent patch lands. The maintainer path is to pass the existing `skipAtIfAlreadyRan` guard through normal due scans, add/keep regression coverage for completed `at` jobs with past `nextRunAtMs`, and verify transient-error retry behavior still works.
