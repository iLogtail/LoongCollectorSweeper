## Summary

Keep open. Current main still matches the reported DAVE-disabled pre-Ready join-abort path: Discord voice passes `daveEncryption: false` through to `@discordjs/voice`, waits 15s for `VoiceConnectionStatus.Ready`, destroys the connection on failure, and only records an OpenClaw voice session after Ready. The current receive-recovery work is post-Ready DAVE decrypt recovery, not a fix for a voice WebSocket close after Hello and before UDP/Ready. The related TTS issue #67453 is distinct and closed as implemented; the related abort/no-session report #39825 remains open rather than being a shipped fix.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #65039 open until maintainers decide the intended non-DAVE behavior. The likely fix belongs in `extensions/discord/src/voice/manager.ts`: add explicit handling for pre-Ready voice close/abort diagnostics and either retry/fallback with DAVE enabled, deprecate or reject `daveEncryption: false` when Discord requires DAVE, or document it as unsupported. Add regression coverage around the initial Ready failure path and update the Discord voice docs/config hints once the product behavior is chosen.
