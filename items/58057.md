## Summary

Current `main` does not implement `dmPolicy: "dynamic"` or a pre-allowlist `message:authorize` hook. The repo also frames channel auth as plugin-owned and documents a single-trust-boundary security model, but that is not strong enough to auto-close a recent, coherent feature request without explicit maintainer scope judgment.
