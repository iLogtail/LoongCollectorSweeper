## Summary

Current main still does not implement the requested wildcard candidate behavior. `rankShortTermPromotionCandidates()` has no `wildcardRatio` or equivalent sampler, filters out candidates below `minScore`, and the deep dreaming caller wires only the existing strict thresholds. Related dreaming fixes such as #68882 improved accumulated signal handling, but they do not add random sub-threshold promotion candidates.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open for memory-core product judgment and implementation. A concrete path would be to add a bounded, testable wildcard policy to deep promotion: collect candidates that pass hard gates but miss `minScore`, sample or deterministically select a small ratio, wire it through scheduled deep dreaming and manual promotion if desired, and document whether the policy is internal-only or configurable.
