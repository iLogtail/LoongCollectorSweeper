## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still applies `minSecurity(configuredSecurity, requestedSecurity ?? configuredSecurity)` in the exec tool, so a model-supplied `security` argument can still downgrade operator-configured exec policy. The PR's proposed regression coverage is also not present on `main`, and the discussion shows the author already iterated to a safer final approach rather than abandoning the fix.
