## Summary

Keep open. Current main has partial coverage: isolated cron jobs can now use a hard `toolsAllow` allowlist, and sub-agents have global/default restriction layers, but the requested per-spawn/session `toolPolicy` surface for `sessions_spawn` is still not present. The issue is concrete, recent, non-protected, and not fully implemented by current main.
