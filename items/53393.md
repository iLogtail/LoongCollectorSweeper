## Summary

Current `main` does not implement this PR’s broader config-derived cache invalidation, and the PR still has a plausible unresolved correctness bug in `context.ts` from the posted review. Conservative cleanup should leave it open for maintainer judgment rather than auto-close it.

## Evidence
