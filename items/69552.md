## Summary

PR #69552 is superseded by the later open PR #70851 from the same author. Current main still needs the behavior, but #69552’s narrow one-line filter is incomplete and #70851 tracks the broader internal-session picker fix, including the active-session exception called out in review.

## Best Possible Solution

Close #69552 as superseded and continue the remaining product work in #70851 or an equivalent maintainer-approved fix: classify internal/runtime sessions at the gateway/session-source boundary, hide that explicit classification from the normal chat picker by default, keep the active internal session selectable when already routed there, and preserve admin/debug visibility for internal rows.
