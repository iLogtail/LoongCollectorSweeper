## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still full-loads and materializes `sessions.json` for both `sessions.list` and `chat.history`. The repo now has cache and maintenance mitigations for oversized stores, but not the storage-layer pagination/lazy-loading that would let us conservatively call this fixed.

## Evidence

- **`sessions.list` still materializes the full store before applying `limit`:** The Gateway handler loads the combined session store, then `listSessionsFromStore()` iterates `Object.entries(store)`, builds rows for every entry, sorts them, and only applies `opts.limit` at the end. That is not paginated or lazy-backed listing.
