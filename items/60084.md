## Summary

Keep open. The issue is recent and well-scoped, and current main still routes memory, task registry, and other file-backed SQLite stores through node:sqlite DatabaseSync with no configurable driver, better-sqlite3 fallback, or lock-probe fallback. I could not live-reproduce the ARM64/Kubernetes fcntl failure in this environment, but the requested mitigation is not implemented on main.
