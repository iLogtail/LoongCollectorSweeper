## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` already covers the Google Chat logging concern through a newer centralized auth-rejection path, but the Zalo half of the PR is not implemented on `main`: `extensions/zalo/src/monitor.ts` still defaults `mediaMaxMb` to 5 MB, and Zalo tests still assert the 5 MB behavior.
