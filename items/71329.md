## Summary

Keep #71329 open. Current main still exposes `messages.statusReactions.enabled` and has the Slack lifecycle reaction controller, but Slack activation remains coupled to `ackReaction`/`shouldAckReaction()`, so the PR’s central behavior is not implemented on main.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the PR open and either merge or adapt its narrow Slack prepare change: let `messages.statusReactions.enabled === true` activate the lifecycle controller independently of `ackReaction`, preserve `enabled: false` as an explicit disable, preserve the existing ackReaction scope-gated path for backwards compatibility, and add/adjust the targeted prepare tests including the missing `ackReactionPromise` assertion from the bot review.
