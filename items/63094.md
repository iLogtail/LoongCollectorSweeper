## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still limits Control UI chat attachments to images, sends them as `type: "image"`, and the gateway drops non-image attachments during parsing. That means the requested Word/PDF/text upload capability is not implemented on `main`, and the reported no-send/no-error behavior remains plausible rather than obsolete.

## Evidence

  - file: [ui/src/ui/chat/attachment-support.ts:1](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/cf858258c71701ef62dddf88aa777873a4962046/ui/src/ui/chat/attachment-support.ts#L1)

  - file: [ui/src/ui/views/chat.ts:1205](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/cf858258c71701ef62dddf88aa777873a4962046/ui/src/ui/views/chat.ts#L1205)
