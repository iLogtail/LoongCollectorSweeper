## Summary

Keep open. Current main still treats mixed text ending in `NO_REPLY` as visible mixed content: exact silent-token payloads are suppressed, but text before a trailing token is stripped-and-delivered rather than suppressing the whole response. The linked PRs #42873 and #46626 are open/unmerged and describe the same remaining work, with #46626 noting additional delivery paths beyond the first normalization change.
