## Summary

Current `main` already has a dedicated ACP/background-task registry that records spawned runs, transitions terminal deliveries out of `pending`, falls back to session-queued delivery when direct channel delivery fails, and has regression tests for both failed delivery and one-time terminal announcement behavior. That matches the zombie repeat-announcement report closely enough to close as implemented on `main`.

## Evidence

- **Terminal ACP delivery is no longer left pending:** `maybeDeliverTaskTerminalUpdate` only runs for `deliveryStatus === "pending"`, suppresses duplicate terminal delivery, and always transitions the task to `delivered`, `session_queued`, `failed`, `parent_missing`, or `not_applicable` after the first completion attempt.
