## Summary

Current `main` does not contain the PR's fix. The bound-route lookup in `resolveSubagentCompletionOrigin` still prefers `threadId` over the chat-derived conversation id, `resolveAnnounceOrigin` still returns the merged session/requester context without a final requester-thread guard, and Telegram success logs still omit `message_thread_id`. The linked issue discussion for #52217 also gives a concrete stale-`lastThreadId` repro, so this remains a plausible unresolved bug rather than cleanup candidate noise.
