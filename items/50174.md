## Summary

Keep open. Current main has substantial Telegram polling hardening and now accepts the reported per-channel healthMonitor config keys, but #50174 also reports an indefinite Discord disconnect/restart loop confirmed by the reporter. I did not find current source, docs, tests, or a canonical related item proving that Discord behavior is fixed or superseded.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #50174 open unless maintainers split it. The Telegram half appears largely addressed by shipped polling/network hardening, but the Discord half needs either a targeted fix for native-Windows disconnect/restart loops, a reproducible current-main diagnostic request, or a narrower canonical Discord issue that explicitly tracks the remaining loop.
