## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` adds session-delta gating and targeted session refreshes, but it still clears and rewrites all indexed chunks for a changed `session.jsonl` file. That means the reported full delete/reinsert pattern remains for modified transcripts, even though sync frequency is now throttled.

## Evidence

- **Session sync is now thresholded, not append-only:** `scheduleSessionDirty` debounces transcript updates for 5 seconds, and `processSessionDeltaBatch` only marks a session dirty once `sync.sessions.deltaBytes` or `sync.sessions.deltaMessages` is exceeded. This reduces sync frequency but does not store a byte offset or append-only cursor.
