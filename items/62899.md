## Summary

Current `main` now exposes and documents an agent-accessible session-level model reset path. `session_status(model="default")` resolves as a reset, clears the stored override, and marks a live model switch pending; the lower-level `sessions.patch` reset path (`model: null`) is also implemented and tested. That addresses the issue’s stated lack of a reliable reset path, even though the original transient-finalization workflow itself was not re-run here.

## Evidence
