## Summary

Keep open. Current main still recommends and applies `before_prompt_build` `prependSystemContext`, and the new empty-output recovery on main is explicitly limited to `stopReason="error"`. The reported `stopReason=stop` + empty content case remains out of scope in tests, so there is no strong evidence that #70868 is fixed on current `main`.
