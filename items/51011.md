## Summary

Keep PR #51011 open. Current main still does not include the proposed iOS Privacy & Access settings section, does not add the Contacts/Calendar/Reminders usage-description keys, and does not provide an equivalent in-app permission-management surface. Related PR #50998 is narrower first-use Contacts/Calendar prompting work and does not supersede this Settings UX or Reminders coverage.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #51011 open as a focused iOS app feature PR. The best path is to review and refine PrivacyAccessSectionView, keep Info.plist and project.yml usage-description metadata aligned, coordinate any overlapping Contacts/Calendar prompt behavior with #50998, and verify Contacts, Calendar write-only/full-access, and Reminders states on a real device or simulator before merge.
