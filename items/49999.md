## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` already includes the hook-session rebinding portion of this draft PR, but it does not include the `ANNOUNCE_SKIP` fallback/suppression changes, and the remaining generic outbound suppression approach would regress explicit `send`/`sendMessage` flows for a literal `ANNOUNCE_SKIP` message.
