## Summary

Current `main` does not show strong evidence that the Feishu/Lark tool-step card UI is fixed. Core OpenClaw does fire `after_tool_call` with session context, but the Feishu channel still only wires streaming/reasoning callbacks and does not consume the newer structured tool-progress callbacks (`onToolStart`, `onItemEvent`, `onCommandOutput`, `onPatchSummary`) that would power a dedicated tool-steps area.
