## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` and the latest shipped release still intentionally default an unset `session.reset` policy to `daily` at `4`, and the behavior is covered by both unit and session-level tests. This issue is therefore not implemented, not obsolete, and not stale; it remains a live product/UX decision about whether unset should instead mean no automatic reset or at least be surfaced more clearly.
