## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement a `transform_context` hook or an equivalent per-LLM-call message rewrite seam for plugins, and the PR discussion surfaced a real policy gap: as proposed, the new hook would bypass existing prompt-injection/conversation-access guardrails unless the hook policy model is extended alongside it.

## Evidence
