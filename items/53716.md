## Summary

Current `main` already covers much of the PR's startup-diagnostics goal with diagnostic stability bundles, but the watchdog feature is still not on `main`, and the submitted watchdog implementation has at least one blocking shipping gap plus a lifecycle bug. That means no allowed close reason applies, and the PR may still contain salvageable work.
