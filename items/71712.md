## Summary

Keep open. Current main has an owner-only agent cron tool, but it is the existing broad cron.add/update/remove surface, not the RFC's narrower gateway-enforced scheduling API. The requested hardening remains meaningfully missing: no playbook allowlist, no required activeUntil for recurring agent-created jobs, no per-agent quota, and no non-forgeable createdBy provenance field in the cron job contract. The AS76 comment reinforces that this matters for multi-agent orchestration rather than making the report obsolete.
