## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has subagent limits and timeouts, but it does not implement the requested failure-count/cooldown circuit breaker or expose breaker state in config/status surfaces. The issue is recent, concrete, and supported by a real-world cost report, so conservative cleanup should not auto-close it.

## Evidence
