## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `84dc9f12f1b9e36f7bfc9bea438bce3852ed9dd5` still does not apply the PR's startup orphan-self-lock cleanup, and the PR discussion leaves a plausible lock-reclamation race unresolved in the proposed patch.
