## Summary

Keep open. Current main has the earlier Moonshot/Kimi tool-call ID fix and optional `thinking.keep="all"` forwarding, but I did not find a Moonshot/Kimi replay repair that preserves or synthesizes top-level `reasoning_content` after LCM/context-engine assembly or cross-model fallback. The report is fresh, has concrete error indices and reproduction details, and remains actionable in OpenClaw even though the linked lossless-claw issue may also need a fix.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #71491 open. Best path: add a Moonshot-owned replay/payload repair, likely in the Moonshot thinking stream wrapper or provider replay hook, that preserves converted `reasoning_content` when present and fills a safe placeholder for assistant tool-call turns when Kimi thinking replay requires it. Cover both same-provider long-history/context-engine assembly and cross-model fallback with focused tests, analogous to the existing DeepSeek/Venice `reasoning_content` replay coverage.
