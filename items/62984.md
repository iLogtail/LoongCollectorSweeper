## Summary

Current `main` contains the packaging/update safeguards that address this report: the `#63065` follow-up shipped in `v2026.4.9`, the packaged postinstall now skips destructive `dist/` pruning when its inventory is missing/invalid, prepack writes that inventory before packing, and the updater verifies the installed package contents before restart.
