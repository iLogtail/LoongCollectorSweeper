## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still intentionally keeps the `openclaw/plugin-sdk` root runtime surface minimal and does not re-export `stringEnum`, `optionalStringEnum`, `jsonResult`, or `formatErrorMessage`. Those helpers are available from focused subpaths (`plugin-sdk/core`, `plugin-sdk/channel-actions`, `plugin-sdk/error-runtime`, etc.), and the docs now explicitly direct plugin authors away from the root barrel. That means the reported third-party breakage via `openclaw/plugin-sdk` is not implemented/fixed on `main`; it remains an unresolved compatibility/design issue rather than something safe to close.

## Evidence
