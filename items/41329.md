## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not show a landed, issue-specific fix that prunes alert-producing heartbeat tool turns out of the parent session transcript, and the existing coverage only proves append-only transcript behavior plus generic tool-result repair. In a read-only review, that is not strong enough evidence to close #41329.
