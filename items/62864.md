## Summary

Current `main` now handles this LM Studio cold-start pattern in two places: it preloads LM Studio models before inference, and the embedded runner retries one same-model idle-timeout when the first attempt produced no visible output. That matches the reported regression where the first post-restart turn times out but later attempts succeed.
