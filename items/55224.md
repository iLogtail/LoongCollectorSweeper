## Summary

Keep open. Current main improves diagnostics and has targeted tool-argument repair paths, but I did not find high-confidence evidence that the reported long-session Telegram/gateai parameter drops for exec, write, and sessions_spawn are fixed end to end.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open and narrow the next maintainer step to a reproducible fixture: capture or synthesize the gateai/Telegram long-session stream for `exec`, `write`, and `sessions_spawn`, including raw tool-call deltas and final tool args. Then either fix the implicated transport/repair gate or document the provider limitation, with regression coverage proving required params survive long-session context pressure.
