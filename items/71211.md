## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still builds foreground `exec` results from raw aggregated stdout/stderr, only binary/control-character sanitization is applied before aggregation, and replay logic forwards tool-result text back into model-visible `function_call_output` content. I found a mitigation that strips `toolResult.details`, but not tool-result `content`, so this issue does not appear implemented or obsolete on `main`.
