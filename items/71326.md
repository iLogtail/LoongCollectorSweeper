## Summary

Keep open. The report is detailed, current, and not maintainer-authored, and current `main` still matches the issue’s described exec shape: gateway-host exec launches through a shell subprocess per call, while docs/changelog confirm the cited 2026.4.20 YOLO/preflight change. I found no evidence on `main` that this stale-read report is fixed, obsolete, or disproven strongly enough for a conservative close.
