## Summary

Keep #51088 open. Current main has message-received hook surfaces and the BlueBubbles iMessage path already supports ack reactions/read receipts, but the requested native `imessage`/`imsg` channel still does not wire `messages.ackReactionScope`, tapbacks, or read receipts into its inbound processing path. The issue is recent, concrete, and not superseded by a narrower canonical item in the provided context.

## Evidence
