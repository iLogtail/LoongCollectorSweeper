## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still generates auto-reply TTS audio under the preferred OpenClaw tmp root and still treats that whole tmp root as an allowed outbound local-media root, so this hardening follow-up is not implemented on `main` and is not safely stale/obsolete.

## Evidence

- **TTS still writes reply audio under preferred tmp root:** `textToSpeech()` still resolves the preferred tmp dir, creates `tts-*` under it, writes `voice-*`, and returns that path; `maybeApplyTtsToPayload()` then forwards `result.audioPath` as reply `mediaUrl`. That means the reply pipeline still depends on tmp-root TTS paths on current `main`.
