## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not show a transport-level fix for this Kimi streaming-order failure: the bundled Kimi provider still installs its own stream wrapper on top of `streamSimple`, provider-owned wrappers take precedence over the boundary-aware fallback path, and the exact `message_start`/`message_stop` failure is only sanitized into friendlier UI copy rather than recovered. The report is also still actionable because the bundled Kimi catalog continues to accept the legacy `k2p5` model id mentioned in the issue logs.

## Evidence

  - file: [extensions/kimi-coding/index.ts:106](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/c65aa1d2a6e573d49619c4dd0698a76010476e7d/extensions/kimi-coding/index.ts#L106)
