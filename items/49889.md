## Summary

Keep open. Current main has partial hook parity for preview-finalized Telegram replies, but the central request remains: operators still cannot clearly distinguish sent vs preview-finalized vs preview-retained vs hook-canceled vs transport-failed vs skipped from the public/internal delivery event shape or documented observability surface.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open and implement an additive delivery outcome surface at the Telegram dispatch boundary: structured logs and optional hook/context metadata for sent, preview-finalized, preview-retained, hook-canceled, transport-failed, and skipped, with messageId when known and docs/tests for both preview and fresh-send paths.
