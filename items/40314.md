## Summary

Current `main` still drops `event.messages` from internal `message:received` and `message:sent` hooks even though the hooks docs say those messages are sent to the user. This PR targets a real behavior gap, but it should stay open because the fix is not on `main` and the review discussion identifies unresolved correctness issues in the proposed patch.

## Evidence
