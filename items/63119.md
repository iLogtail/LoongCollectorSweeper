## Summary

Current main already covers the request well enough: normal compaction keeps transcript history on disk, session resets archive prior transcript files as `*.jsonl.reset.<timestamp>`, and retention/cleanup is configurable through `session.maintenance.resetArchiveRetention` and enforced by session-store maintenance.

## Evidence

- **Compaction does not delete transcript history:** The compaction docs state that older turns are summarized into a compact entry, the summary is saved in the session transcript, and the full conversation history stays on disk. That contradicts the issue’s current-behavior claim for normal compaction.

  - sha: [cf858258c717](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/cf858258c71701ef62dddf88aa777873a4962046)
