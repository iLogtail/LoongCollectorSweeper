## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` already filters heartbeat/cron/session noise and deep dreaming uses weighted, recency-aware scoring, so parts of the report are stale against the code/docs. But the session-corpus ingestion loop still walks a stable sorted file list under a fixed sweep budget with per-file caps and persisted cursors, with no rotation or recency prioritization. That makes the reported session-corpus stall plausible, so there is not strong evidence to close this item as implemented or cannot reproduce.
