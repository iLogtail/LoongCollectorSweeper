## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not restore general recursive skill scanning. It has a narrow heuristic for repo-style `dir/skills/*` layouts, but the loader still only enumerates immediate child directories under the resolved skills root, and there is no scan-depth config or `manifest.yaml`-based recursive stop behavior.
