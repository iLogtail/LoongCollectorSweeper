## Summary

Current main has stronger user-visible sanitizers for streamed/final assistant text, but the underlying transcript-divergence part of #38291 is not clearly resolved. Thinking tags are promoted at write-time, yet raw shadow/tool-call text is still stripped only on extraction paths, while chat-history and sessions-history surfaces largely pass persisted assistant text through truncation/redaction without the shared assistant-visible sanitizer.

## Evidence

## Risks / Open Questions

- Closing as implemented would overstate the fix: current main clearly cleans visible extraction/delivery paths, but the stored-transcript divergence remains plausible and at least some history readers still expose persisted text without the shared sanitizer.
- The open issue scope is broader than Discord-only thinking tags; current evidence does not show a strong end-to-end proof that raw tagged/tool-call shadow text can no longer surface from transcript-backed paths.
