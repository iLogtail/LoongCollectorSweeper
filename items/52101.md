## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not contain this PR’s compat/root export expansion, and the current SDK contract intentionally keeps `openclaw/plugin-sdk` tiny while treating `openclaw/plugin-sdk/compat` as a deprecated-but-still-supported migration surface. The item is therefore not implemented on `main`, not obsolete, and not strong enough to auto-close; it needs maintainer judgment on the right compatibility fix shape.
