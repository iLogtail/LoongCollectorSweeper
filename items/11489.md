## Summary

Keep #11489 open. Current main still has DM/node pairing only, while group handling remains allow/block via `open | disabled | allowlist`; there is no group pairing state, owner approval flow, group-join notification, or pending read-only group agent behavior. Related #42400 and #8495 remain open and cover narrower adjacent work, not the requested cross-channel group pairing flow.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #11489 open for maintainer/product direction. The best implementation is an additive core/channel contract: add a `groupPolicy: "pairing"` config/schema/docs path, persist pending group approvals keyed by channel/account/group ID, add CLI approve/reject semantics, notify the owner on group join or first-seen unconfigured group, teach Telegram/WhatsApp and other group-capable channels to honor the state, and decide whether pending read-only observation belongs in the same change or a narrower follow-up coordinated with #42400 and #8495.
