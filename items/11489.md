## Summary

Keep #11489 open. Current main still has DM/node pairing only; group policy remains `open | disabled | allowlist`, Telegram and WhatsApp group paths allow or block rather than queue owner approval, and the CLI pairing store/commands approve sender codes into DM allowlists rather than group chat IDs. Related #8495 and #42400 are useful but narrower and still open, so neither supersedes the requested cross-channel group pairing flow.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #11489 open for maintainer/product direction. The likely path is an additive core/channel contract change: extend group policy config/schema/docs, add pending group approval state keyed by channel/account/group ID, add CLI approve/reject semantics, teach Telegram/WhatsApp and other group-capable channels to notify the owner on join or first-seen group, and decide whether pending read-only observation is handled through a separate no-reply/sandboxed-reader route or a narrower follow-up.
