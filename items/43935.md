## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still plans and executes channel-wide hot reloads for channel config-prefix matches, even though the channel manager supports optional account-scoped start/stop. I found no landed change in the relevant files between the latest release `v2026.4.22` (`00bd2cf7a376f1fba26291c6c4766f1f15cbdfa5`) and current `main` (`50e36983bb2d827adc617282c090c0a0359c6eee`).
