## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement the proposed opt-in `injectAgentHeader` / `X-OpenClaw-Agent` provider behavior, and the PR discussion surfaces a plausible correctness gap around normalized provider IDs. I do not have strong evidence for any auto-close path.
