## Summary

Keep open. Current main has generic duplicate tool-call ID repair, but the bundled Moonshot provider still opts out of that sanitizer, and its tests/docs codify preserving Kimi IDs unchanged. The issue is recent, has a concrete WhatsApp group-chat reproduction, and the related open reports do not clearly supersede this channel-specific failure.
