## Summary

Current main already addresses the reported Discord stale-socket behavior strongly enough to close: stale-socket recovery now depends on provider-proven transport activity, quiet Discord channels are no longer restarted just because no app messages arrived, the health-monitor restart scope is channel/account-level rather than a full gateway restart, and the Discord reconnect-exhausted crash loop around these restarts was separately fixed and shipped.

## Evidence

- **Health-monitor restart scope is channel/account only:** The monitor logs `[channelId:accountId] health-monitor: restarting` and then calls `channelManager.stopChannel()` followed by `channelManager.startChannel()` for that specific account; it does not trigger a gateway-process restart path here.

- **Stale-socket now requires transport-liveness data:** `stale-socket` is only evaluated when `snapshot.connected === true` and `lastTransportActivityAt != null`. Without provider-proven transport activity, the health policy returns healthy instead of forcing a stale-socket restart.

## Close Comment

Closing after a Codex review of current `main`. OpenClaw now gates `stale-socket` recovery on provider-proven transport activity (`src/gateway/channel-health-policy.ts:117`), and the regression coverage explicitly verifies that connected channels without transport-liveness data are skipped instead of being restarted (`src/gateway/channel-health-monitor.test.ts:543`). That shipped in `v2026.4.22` (`CHANGELOG.md:116`, `CHANGELOG.md:278`). The restart scope is also channel/account-level, not a full gateway-process restart: the health monitor calls `channelManager.stopChannel()` / `startChannel()` for the affected `[channelId:accountId]` entry (`src/gateway/channel-health-monitor.ts:158`). Separately, the Discord reconnect-exhausted crash-loop around these restarts was fixed in `2026.3.28` (`CHANGELOG.md:1914`, `CHANGELOG.md:2003`). If you still see BOOT.md notifications on `v2026.4.22` or newer, please open a fresh issue with current logs that show the exact restart path.
