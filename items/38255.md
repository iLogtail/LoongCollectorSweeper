## Summary

Current `main` does not implement this PR's Telegram config surface. The runtime still hardcodes the Telegram preview defaults, and the Telegram config schema/types on `main` do not expose `channels.telegram.streamThrottleMs` or `channels.telegram.minInitialChars`.
