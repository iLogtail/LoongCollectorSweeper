## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still sends a restart-sentinel outbound notice whenever it can resolve a delivery route, and there is no matching config/schema knob to disable that notification. The original wrong-channel symptom appears partially addressed by newer session-scoped routing, but the requested opt-out is not implemented.

## Evidence

- **Restart notice still auto-delivers:** `scheduleRestartSentinelWake` unconditionally enqueues the wake event, resolves the origin route, and calls `deliverRestartSentinelNotice(...)` when `channel` and `to` are available. There is no `enabled`/`notify` guard in that path.
  - file: [src/gateway/server-restart-sentinel.ts:360](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/34896839ba22f3a71a3d5a8fb4ca5f4c6989a534/src/gateway/server-restart-sentinel.ts#L360)
