## Summary

Current main still appears to have the reported behavior under the default configuration: `sessions_list` fetches gateway sessions, then filters them through session-tool visibility. The default visibility is `tree`, and `tree` rejects same-agent sessions that are neither the requester session nor spawned children. The issue's requested default visibility for active group/channel sessions therefore remains meaningfully unresolved unless the operator explicitly configures `tools.sessions.visibility` to `agent` or `all`.
