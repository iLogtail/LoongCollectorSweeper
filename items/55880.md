## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` appears to have fixed the macOS LaunchAgent recovery part of the report, but the item as filed is broader and is not fully implemented. Package-manager self-update still runs direct global installs and still treats the post-update `doctor --non-interactive` pass as blocking, and cron delivery reporting still explicitly preserves a split between `delivered` and `deliveryStatus`, so there is not strong evidence to close this as fully implemented or obsolete.
