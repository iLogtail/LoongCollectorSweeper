## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still hardcodes the Claude live-session stdout cap at 256 KB, aborts on that limit in both the line parser and stdout buffer path, and exposes no config/schema knob for operators to raise it. A related PR exists, but it was closed unmerged and its commit is not present on `main`.
