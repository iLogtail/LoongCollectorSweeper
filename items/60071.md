## Summary

Keep open. Current main still treats a resolved channel `startAccount` promise as channel exit and schedules auto-restart, while the Twitch plugin's `startAccount` awaits `monitorTwitchProvider()` and that monitor returns `{ stop }` immediately after registering its abort handler. The same lifecycle pattern reported in #60071 therefore still exists on main.

## Evidence

- **Twitch startAccount resolves after monitor returns:** `twitchPlugin.gateway.startAccount` awaits `monitorTwitchProvider(...)` and then exits; it does not keep the account task pending until `ctx.abortSignal` fires.
  - file: [extensions/twitch/src/plugin.ts:169](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/b64bfc5d9abf078217a0a63c25d4c2bcbf4e6fab/extensions/twitch/src/plugin.ts#L169)
