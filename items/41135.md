## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still implements auth-profile selection as `auth.order` plus round-robin/cooldown failover with session stickiness; it does not expose the requested provider-pool routing layer, routing strategies, per-task/non-sticky modes, or per-profile concurrency/in-flight controls.

## Evidence

- **Docs still describe session-sticky profile reuse:** `docs/concepts/model-failover.md:82-116` says profile choice is driven by `auth.order`, configured/stored profiles, round-robin by `usageStats.lastUsed`, and then pinned per session until reset/compaction/cooldown.
  - file: [docs/concepts/model-failover.md:82](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/e94c0bf51542fe518e49ad4e2ee29882891b5603/docs/concepts/model-failover.md#L82)

No pool strategy abstraction or policy enum is present.
