## Summary

Keep open. Current main still exposes a single exclusive `plugins.slots.memory` selector, not purpose-specific `memory.recall`, `memory.compaction`, `memory.capture`, or `memory.userModel` slots. Recent work appears to address narrower coexistence cases such as memory-core dreaming and additive memory-adjacent prompt/corpus supplements, but the requested multi-slot memory architecture and per-agent slot taxonomy remain missing.
