## Summary

Keep open. I did not find strong evidence that current `main` fixes or obsoletes this report. Telegram still runs through its own routed session/workspace path and skill snapshot path, vLLM is still treated as a generic OpenAI-compatible `openai-completions` backend, and the release-to-main history here does not show a Telegram/vLLM parity fix.
