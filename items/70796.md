## Summary

Current `main` already exposes multi-session switching in the chat header and from the Sessions page, but it does not implement the specific requested UX of a conversations dropdown/list under the Chat item in the left sidebar. This remains a valid frontend enhancement rather than something that is clearly already implemented.
