Closing as implemented on `main` after a Codex review. The report matches a packaged-install startup regression family that has since been addressed: v2026.4.20 shipped bundled-plugin packaged-install repair before import (`appcast.xml:207`, `appcast.xml:269`, also `CHANGELOG.md:410`), and current `main` additionally carries the bundled-plugin Jiti loader fix for global-install discovery hangs (`CHANGELOG.md:85`, `src/plugins/loader.ts:451`) with regression coverage in `src/plugins/loader.jiti-filename.test.ts:51`.
