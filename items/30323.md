## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not already implement the PR’s blanket tab-response URL enrichment, and the remaining design still needs maintainer judgment because URL emission here is policy-sensitive and the proposed post-action lookup can misattribute URLs after target loss/tab close flows.
