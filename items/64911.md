## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still logs `ready (...)` before post-listen sidecars begin, uses `/readyz` as the stricter startup signal, and only startup-gates `chat.history`/`models.list`. `openclaw cron run` still goes straight through normal WebSocket RPC, so the reported restart-time handshake race is not clearly fixed on `main`.
