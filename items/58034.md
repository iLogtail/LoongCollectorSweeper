## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` includes the narrower DNS-before-proxy baseline, but it still does not ship this PR's explicit `tools.web.fetch.useTrustedEnvProxy` surface, its `web_fetch` opt-in wiring/docs, or the pre-dispatch hostname-policy hardening in the trusted env-proxy branch.
