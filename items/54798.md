## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has cron overlap protection for the same job, but it does not expose the requested shared atomic counter, `session kv incr` primitive, or a `sessions_spawn` mutex/exclusive option for concurrent sub-agents.

## Evidence

- **Session tools on main do not include a KV/counter primitive:** The documented session tool surface is limited to `sessions_list`, `sessions_history`, `sessions_send`, `sessions_spawn`, `sessions_yield`, `subagents`, and `session_status`; there is no `session kv` or atomic counter tool.
  - file: [docs/concepts/session-tool.md:13](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/a6aa151653ab478e9aa23e95c6446c41adc4fce1/docs/concepts/session-tool.md#L13)

- **`sessions_spawn` has no lock or exclusivity parameter:** The documented `sessions_spawn` params are `task`, `label`, `agentId`, `model`, `thinking`, `runTimeoutSeconds`, `thread`, `mode`, `cleanup`, `sandbox`, and `context`. There is no `exclusive`, mutex, or shared-counter option.
  - file: [docs/tools/subagents.md:88](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/a6aa151653ab478e9aa23e95c6446c41adc4fce1/docs/tools/subagents.md#L88)
