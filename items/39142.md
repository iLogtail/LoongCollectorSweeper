## Summary

Keep #39142 open. Current main partially mitigates the raw-error symptom with built-in generic/direct-chat failure copy and silent group/channel handling, but it still has no configurable `messages.modelExhausted` or equivalent user-facing exhaustion-message surface. Pure billing and pure transient rate-limit fallback exhaustion still use fixed built-in text, and the messages config/schema remains strict without a custom exhaustion field.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open until core either adds a supported configurable exhaustion-message surface or a maintainer explicitly chooses a different product direction. A complete fix should add the field to `MessagesConfig`, `MessagesSchema`, generated config help/labels/reference docs, and `runAgentTurnWithFallback`, with tests for pure billing, pure transient rate-limit, and the mixed billing/rate-limit policy decision.
