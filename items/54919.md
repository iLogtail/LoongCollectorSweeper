## Summary

Current `main` appears to have fixed the original `agents.defaults.thinkingDefault` validation/runtime gap, but the issue body requests both `agents.defaults.thinkingDefault` and `agents.defaults.reasoningDefault`. `thinkingDefault` is now wired up; `reasoningDefault` still is not. Conservative cleanup says keep this open unless the scope is narrowed or split.
