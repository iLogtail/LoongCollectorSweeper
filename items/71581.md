## Summary

Current `main` does not implement this request. Exec-event and cron-event heartbeat wakes still target the literal originating `sessionKey`, while DM routing with `session.dmScope="main"` collapses direct chats to the agent main session. That leaves a real, coherent feature gap rather than something already fixed or safe to auto-close.

## Evidence
