## Summary

Keep #51881 open. Current main still routes the sniffed MIME through `parseMessageWithAttachments()` unchanged, so a `file-type` result of `image/apng` would still become the model image `mimeType` and then the Anthropic `source.media_type`. The related discussion and PRs are useful, but the fix is not shipped on main.

## Best Possible Solution

Land a focused Gateway/media fix that normalizes `image/apng` to `image/png` before choosing `finalMime` and before offload support checks, with a regression test in `src/gateway/chat-attachments.test.ts` that forces the sniffed MIME to `image/apng` and verifies the final image/offloaded MIME is `image/png`. Then close #51881 and any redundant open PRs against the landed implementation.
