## Summary

Keep open. The related #66505 appears to cover direct Ollama Cloud support, but #71697 reports a separate native Ollama agent/Gateway replay failure. Current main still forwards native Ollama chat history without an Ollama-specific user/assistant alternation repair, and the shared OpenAI-compatible replay policy does not apply to `api: "ollama"`.
