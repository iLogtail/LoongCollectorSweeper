## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has partial overlap only: plugin-level `session_start` lifecycle hooks and a runtime startup-context prelude for bare `/new` and `/reset`, but no `SESSION_START.md` workspace hook, no `agents.defaults.sessionStart.*` config, and no built-in silent agentic startup turn that runs on every new session (including automatic idle/daily rollovers).

## Evidence

- **No internal session-start workspace hook surface:** The internal hooks docs list command, compaction, bootstrap, gateway, and message events, but no `session:start`/`session_start` internal event. Bundled hooks are limited to `session-memory`, `bootstrap-extra-files`, `command-logger`, and `boot-md`.
