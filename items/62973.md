## Summary

Current `main` already has a broader external-content prompt-injection defense that supersedes this PR's transport-layer wrapper approach, with stronger anti-spoofing and documented coverage for fetched/untrusted content.

## Evidence

- **Shared external-content wrapper on main:** `wrapExternalContent()` now wraps untrusted content with unique `<<<EXTERNAL_UNTRUSTED_CONTENT ...>>>` markers plus an explicit warning, and the same module sanitizes spoofed boundary markers and model special tokens before the model sees them.
