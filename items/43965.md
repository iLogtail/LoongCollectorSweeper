## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has related subagent-completion capture work, but it does not include this PR's extractor changes, and current regression coverage explicitly rejects one of the PR's key fallback behaviors. I do not have strong evidence that #43755 is already fixed on `main` well enough to close this PR.
