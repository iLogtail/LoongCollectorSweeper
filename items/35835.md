## Summary

Keep open. Current main has audio support in the inbound media-understanding pipeline and general MIME helpers, but the `read` tool path remains image-only and still falls back to UTF-8 text for non-image binaries. This is not a conservative close as implemented, cannot-reproduce, duplicate, or ClawHub-only scope.
