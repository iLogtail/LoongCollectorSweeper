## Summary

Keep open. Current main only exposes and sorts sessions by `updatedAt`; it does not implement the requested `lastMeaningfulActivityAt` model, reason label, or parent rollup sorting. The sub-agent label side concern was split to #51030, but that does not supersede the central sorting request.
