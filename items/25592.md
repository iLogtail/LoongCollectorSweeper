## Summary

Keep #25592 open. Current main has meaningful commentary/final_answer hardening, but it still preserves auto-delivery for unphased assistant text before tool execution, still feeds accumulated assistantTexts into final payloads, and has no replyMode/tool-only or configurable final-only channel policy. The latest issue discussion also explicitly keeps this as the broader canonical bug after subset fixes.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #25592 open as the canonical product bug until OpenClaw implements or explicitly rejects a channel-safe policy such as opt-in replyMode/tool-only explicit sends, final-only channel delivery, or configurable enforceFinalTag for channel sessions. Preserve the shipped commentary-phase hardening, but add focused regression coverage for unphased pre-tool text and final payload accumulation across representative channel delivery paths.
