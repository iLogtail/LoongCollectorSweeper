## Summary

Keep #25592 open. Current main has meaningful commentary/final_answer hardening, but it does not solve the broader bug: ordinary unphased assistant text can still be buffered, emitted as block replies before tool execution, and preserved as final payload input. The issue discussion also treats #25592 as the broader canonical thread after the commentary-phase subset fixes, with fresh reports after those fixes.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #25592 open as the canonical product bug until OpenClaw implements or explicitly rejects a channel-safe policy such as final-only channel delivery, opt-in replyMode/tool-only explicit sends, or configurable enforceFinalTag for channel sessions. Preserve the shipped commentary-phase hardening, but add coverage for unphased pre-tool/intermediate text so channel delivery cannot expose it.
