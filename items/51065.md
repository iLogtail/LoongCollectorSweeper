## Summary

Keep open. Current main still intentionally creates user-visible warning payloads for mutating tool failures, including edit/write-style failures, and Telegram dispatch sends final error payloads through the normal reply path. The open related PR #51091 appears to track a narrower suppression fix, while #51213 was closed unmerged, so there is no strong basis to close #51065 as implemented or superseded.
