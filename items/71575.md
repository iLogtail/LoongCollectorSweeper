## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` intentionally streams Telegram tool-progress lines into the live preview by default, and docs/changelog describe that as shipped behavior, but the issue also reports duplicated/leaked raw tool output in user-visible Telegram messages. That still looks like a plausible product bug or behavior disagreement, not a safe auto-close case.
