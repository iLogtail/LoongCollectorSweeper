## Summary

Current main still hardcodes Feishu card note/footer text as `Agent:`, `Model:`, and `Provider:` and still sends that note through streaming and structured card paths. I found no config/docs option to disable or reformat the footer, and existing tests still assert `note: "Agent: agent"`. Related Feishu footer customization issues remain open, but they are broader feature requests rather than a shipped fix for this concrete Markdown parsing bug.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #59360 open as a concrete Feishu bundled-plugin bug. The likely fix belongs in `extensions/feishu`: either change `resolveCardNote` to a Feishu-safe non-definition-list format, add a documented per-channel/per-account option to disable the note, or both. Cover streaming and non-streaming card paths in `reply-dispatcher.test.ts`, update Feishu config/docs, and coordinate with the broader open footer customization issues #60748 and #66759 rather than closing this specific bug yet.
