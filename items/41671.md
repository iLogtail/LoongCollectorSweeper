## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still applies a retry-time sticky IPv4 fallback for Telegram that hardcodes `autoSelectFamily: false` and `dnsResultOrder: "ipv4first"` after retryable fetch errors, and Telegram media downloads explicitly use that fallback path. That means the reported IPv4-broken/IPv6-working failure mode is not clearly fixed on `main` or in `v2026.4.22`.

## Evidence

- **Fallback policy still hardcodes IPv4-only behavior:** `resolveTelegramTransport()` builds `fallbackDispatcherPolicy` with `autoSelectFamily: false`, `dnsResultOrder: "ipv4first"`, and `forceIpv4: true`, without checking whether the caller explicitly configured different Telegram network settings.
