## Summary

Keep #38836 open. Current main has meaningful restart hardening, including managed restart intent/drain, restart sentinel delivery for one captured session, and internal restart-aborted main-session recovery. But the requested Feishu-visible notification/fan-out for all in-flight Feishu sessions interrupted by a direct `systemctl --user restart openclaw-gateway` path is still not implemented on `edf40ab6`.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this open until OpenClaw either implements generic external-channel restart interruption handling or explicitly documents direct supervisor/systemctl restarts as outside the supported no-loss boundary. The likely implementation belongs in gateway/session recovery: persist affected delivery contexts before shutdown or while marking aborted sessions, then on startup deliver a Feishu-visible interruption notice or resume-and-deliver through the original route, with regression coverage for Feishu or a generic channel delivery contract.
