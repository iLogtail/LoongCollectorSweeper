## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still serializes inbound auto-reply work per session across channels, and active-session messages default to queued followups rather than separate channel lanes or immediate per-message acknowledgements. There are mitigations on `main` such as immediate typing indicators and `steer`/`collect` queue modes, but the core behavior reported in #40140 is still present.

## Evidence
