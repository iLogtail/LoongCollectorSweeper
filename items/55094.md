## Summary

Current `main` still appears to match the report. The image tool loads remote URLs through `loadWebMedia()` without supplying any SSRF override, `loadWebMedia()` forwards that undefined policy to `fetchRemoteMedia()`, and only the web-fetch path has an RFC2544 benchmark-range opt-in. I did not find a corresponding media/image config seam or test covering 198.18.0.0/15 for remote image-tool inputs.

## Evidence

No `ssrfPolicy`, `proxyUrl`, or `trustExplicitProxyDns` is provided on this path.
