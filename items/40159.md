## Summary

Current main already avoids the reported failure mode: provisional subagent sessions created through `sessions.patch` now have a real `sessionId` and `updatedAt`, and `initSessionState()` reuses that fresh entry and carries its stored model/provider overrides forward instead of rebuilding a blank first-turn session.

## Evidence
