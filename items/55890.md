## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` and the shipped `v2026.4.22` release support `autoThreadName`, but only as a Discord channel-level setting under `channels.discord.guilds.<guild>.channels.<channel>` (or wildcard channel entries). The issue’s reported placements at `channels.discord` and `channels.discord.guilds.<guild>` still appear invalid by schema, and the docs/changelog do not explicitly document the correct config path for `autoThreadName`.
