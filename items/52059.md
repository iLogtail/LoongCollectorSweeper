## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement `gateway.auth.scopes`, and the gateway still intentionally strips scopes from device-less token/password sessions outside narrow local-backend exceptions. The PR’s requested behavior is therefore not already shipped or obsolete on `main`.
