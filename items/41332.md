## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not contain the PR's three substantive changes: the gateway still exits via `process.exit`, model fallback still probes and bypasses active cooldowns, and cron startup catch-up still lacks a configurable `startupStaggerMs` delay for immediate missed-job execution.
