## Summary

Keep open. The reported ClawHub-first plugin resolution behavior is still present on current main and is explicitly covered by docs and tests. Main has mitigations such as integrity checks, source metadata, and warnings for non-official ClawHub packages, but those do not fully address the name-squatting/trust concern raised in the issue and comments.
