## Summary

Current `main` already fixes the reported endless unknown-tool loop through the embedded runner's always-on unknown-tool guard and threshold rewrite, while intentionally keeping sandbox error rewriting limited to actual policy blocks. This PR's proposed sandbox-message path is superseded and its masking risk was already called out in review.

## Evidence
