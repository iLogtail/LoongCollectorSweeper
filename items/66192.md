## Summary

Keep open. `main` now ships a 30s TUI streaming watchdog that clears a stuck busy indicator and the changelog says it guards against lost or late final chat events, but the specific late-terminal-event reset logic proposed in this PR is still not present on `main`. Because the landed fix is overlapping rather than behaviorally identical, the evidence is not strong enough for an auto-close.
