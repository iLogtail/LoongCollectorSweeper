## Summary

Keep #51805 open. Current main supports DM scoping and identityLinks only for direct-message peer keys; group/channel sessions still resolve to isolated channel/group keys and ignore identityLinks. The requested per-user shared context across group chats and DMs, with filtering so other group members do not leak into the user's DM context, remains a real product/session-routing gap rather than an implemented feature or safe ClawHub-only cleanup.
