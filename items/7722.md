## Summary

Keep #7722 open. Current main still implements only the narrower `tools.fs.workspaceOnly` workspace guard plus sandbox hardening; it does not accept `tools.fileAccess`, `mode`, `allowedPaths`, or `denyPaths`, and it does not enforce filesystem allow/deny rules for `exec` commands. The provided discussion also includes a same-day maintainer/Codex comment explicitly preserving this scope, with #5948 as the narrower workspace-only related request rather than a full replacement.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #7722 open until OpenClaw either implements a versioned filesystem access policy surface, likely under `tools.fs` or a successor config contract, with deny-wins allow/deny path rules enforced across read/write/edit/apply_patch and a defensible exec strategy, or a maintainer explicitly narrows or rejects that product direction. Treat shipped `tools.fs.workspaceOnly` and sandbox backends as useful partial hardening, not completion of this request.
