## Summary

Keep #59234 open. Current main still does not implement the requested QMD search-mode mitigation: `probeVectorAvailability()` always shells out to `qmd status`, shared status collection still invokes that probe, and QMD embed maintenance is explicitly kept active regardless of retrieval mode. The linked #67113 is related and still open, but it is broader embed-loop work and does not clearly supersede this more specific vector-status-probe report.

## Evidence

- **Vector probe still shells out to qmd status:** `probeVectorAvailability()` unconditionally calls `this.runQmd(["status"])` with a timeout and records `QMD status probe failed` on error; it does not branch on `this.qmd.searchMode === "search"`.
