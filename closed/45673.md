## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` contains the basic Feishu tool precedence swap, but it does not include the reviewed follow-up work from PR #45673 that scopes inherited account preference to Feishu-origin sessions and threads `messageChannel` through the tool-account resolver. The review comments identified a real cross-channel misrouting risk, and that risk is still present in `main`.

## Evidence
